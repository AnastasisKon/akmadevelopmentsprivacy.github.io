## Privacy Policy for Ghost Detect Investigator Pro
Last Updated: July 2026
### Privacy Policy
Ghost Detector Pro ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.
### Information Accessed
Depending on the features you use, the App may request access to:
- Camera
- Microphone
- Bluetooth
- Motion Sensors (Accelerometer and Gyroscope)
- Magnetometer
- Barometer
- Speech Recognition
- Local File Storage
These permissions are used solely to provide the App's functionality.
### Data Collection
The App does not collect personal information.
The App does not require user accounts.
The App does not track users across apps or websites.
The App does not collect analytics or advertising identifiers.
### Data Storage
Sensor recordings, photographs, videos, audio recordings, transcripts, telemetry logs, CSV files, and ZIP archives are stored locally on your device.
No data is automatically uploaded to external servers.
### Data Sharing
The App does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
Data is shared only if you explicitly choose to export files using Apple's standard Share Sheet.
### Third-Party Services
Ghost Detector Pro uses Apple's system frameworks, including technologies such as AVFoundation, Vision, ARKit, CoreMotion, CoreBluetooth, Metal, and Speech Recognition, to provide its features.
The App does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
### Data Security
All recordings and exported files remain under your control on your device unless you choose to share them.
### Children's Privacy
The App is not specifically directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
### Changes to this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page.
